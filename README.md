Service commands:

| :-----------------: | :-------------------: | :------------------: | :---------------: |
|        !help        |         !srv          |         !emb         |       !clear      |

Music commands:

|   Plays music  |  Makes a quick embed  |  Unpause music |  Stops playing music |  Commands the bot to leave the voice  |
| :------------: | :-------------------: | :------------: | :------------------: | :----------------------------------:  |
|      !play     |         !pause        |     !resume    |       !stop          |               !leave                  |
